Instructions

Sync the generator to the grid by matching the grid frequency (50Hz) and phase, by changing the position of the wicket gates.
Once matched, open the wicket gates to start exporting energy to the grid
A few conditions will cause the controls to lock out, e.g. overspeed or running as a motor

Controls

  • "q" - Closes the wicket gates
  • "w" - Opens the wicket gates
  • space - Closes the contactor to connect the generator to the grid
  • Control "R" - Reset the system
